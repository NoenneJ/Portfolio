Website with a learnable experience


Develop a digital platform that introduces users to a specific topic of your choice and is designed to engage the user. The goal is to create an immersive digital environment that facilitates various forms of learning experiences, such as tutorials, videos, quizzes and more. The objective is to provide insights into a self-defined theme while actively involving the user in the learning process.

Process


This project was quite extensive, and I'd like to walk you through the processes I was involved in.

Discover:
During the discovery phase, we focused on finding a topic.
We used brainstorming and card sorting to decide on a theme that was both manageable and interesting.
Once we settled on creating an online dog training website with video content, we delved into researching our competitors, understanding our target audience, and uncovering how people preferred to train their dogs.

Define:
With a better understanding of our project, we were able to define our target audience. Our platform was geared towards introverted individuals living in larger cities who didn't necessarily want to participate in social communities.
We then created personas and developed empathy maps, user stories, and flowcharts to map out our users and how they would engage with our content.

Develop:
In the development phase, we started by creating hi-fi mock-ups in Figma and later translated the design into WordPress Elementor.
We ensured a consistent visual style by following a style guide.
Key features we implemented included a functional login system and content-saving options for non-logged-in users.
We also put our creative hats on and designed various elements, such as dogs, space rockets and backgrounds, in line with our project's theme using Adobe Illustrator.

Deliver:
Before launching we conducted thorough testing. Usability tests were conducted both before and after implementing changes to gauge the improvements. Additionally, we used think-aloud tests with specific tasks for our test participants.
